Gram Vikas Sanstha, Bhandara organization is an inspiration in charitable sector with the inspiration of Mr. Madhukarji Dhas, since 2016, the organization first undertook the task of creating a ray of hope for the poor, tribals, deprived, weaksections of the rural and urban areas, mainly in their difficult life. Along with this, for the 5 years, Saibaba Rural Development Institute, Chandur Railway Dist. has been carrying out the message of Gram Gita of Rashtrasanta Tukadoji Maharaj and Father of the Nation Mahatma Gandhi's concept of village development.
Gram Vikas Sanstha is working with like-minded organizations in 11 districts of vidarbha with the concept of sustainable village development along with Gramseval Lok Abhiyan(Vidarbha) under Amravati.
We are grateful to those who have supported us to further enhance this social work. Your contibution will be invaluable to continue this work vigorously. Know the importance of education as a strong sense of cooperation by cooperating with dignitaries with social intelligence and social vision. It is a request to cooperate in thie work by extending a helping hand as pet the request of this national saint.
1.Guidance and support to people through various schemes and activities to do Health, agriculture, employment and education in rural and urban areas Working with more emphasis.
2.Helping hand activities for orphans, destitute, poor in backward areas.
3.To help the needy, tribal and underprivileged people. Women empowerment, small scale enterprise by providing training for seld employment.
4.Target group of the organization : Orphans, destitute, needy, poor children and single women. Laborers and farmers and Tribal family and family of suicide farmers.
1. Continuously working towards sustainable development for the purpose of overall development in rural and urban areas.
2. To implement helping hand activities for deprived, weak, poor, destitute and tribal families in urban and rural areas.
